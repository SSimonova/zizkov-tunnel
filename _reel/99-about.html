About Me


Sofya Simonova

I’d like to meet you. My name is Sofya Simonova, I am 24 years old, I was born in Moscow, since 2018 I have been living, studying and working in the Czech Republic. I am a participant and laureate of foreign exhibitions. Exhibited in cultural centers: Portugal-spring 2010, Israel-autumn 2010, France-autumn 2011, Holland and Belgium-autumn 2012. I have an art education and two years of photography studies at a higher educational institution in Moscow. «I am a great fighter for a safe life and freedom of expression. Therefore, this project causes me a storm of emotions and a desire to make some changes to improve this tunnel.»
