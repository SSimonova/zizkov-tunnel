Exaples from the world

The Berlin Wall is the most famous example, the wall was built in 1961 to separate East and West Berlin. After the fall, in 1990 artists from all over the world began to paint the wall, 160 paintings, 118 artists from 21 countries of the world. This is the largest exhibition dedicated to freedom and peace.
